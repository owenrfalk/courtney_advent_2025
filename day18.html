Getting Möbi

How I Felt
“I am almost as obsessed with this dog as I am with this lady”
Oh Dear
This was the beginning of us starting our family! Besides Möbi being my soul animal, this was the first time I truly got to see how great it would be to have a family with you. You were there for all of it. The 4am shifts we took to take him out to pee, the shift in lifestyle where we couldn’t leave him alone, trying to keep him from ingesting 5000 acorns on our daily walks. All the hard parts of raising a puppy felt magical knowing that I had such a rock solid partner to rely on in the hard times and rejoice in the stories that no one else would find remotely interesting.
